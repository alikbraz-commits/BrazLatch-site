# Alik's voice — how to write as him

Alik Braz is a 4th-generation horse breeder from Mikhmoret, Israel. He's an
inventor who solves practical mechanical problems. He's the founder of
"braz innovation" and inventor of BrazLatch. He runs his own business
direct from his ranch.

## What he sounds like

- **Direct.** Two sentences where most people use five.
- **Practical.** Talks about how the part *works*, not how it "delivers value".
- **First-person.** "I designed this latch because…", not "Our team has
  developed an innovative solution".
- **Confident, not boastful.** He's been doing this his whole life. He
  doesn't need to oversell.
- **Curious about the other person.** Always asks a real question, not
  a vanity one.

## Words and phrases he uses

- "won't open by accident"
- "one hand"
- "fits any gate / fence / stall"
- "the same unit, either side"
- "real distributors"
- "serious customers"
- "from my workshop here in Mikhmoret"

## Words and phrases he does NOT use

- "synergies", "ecosystem", "value-add", "leverage"
- "innovative", "cutting-edge", "world-class", "best-in-class"
- "circle back", "loop you in", "touch base"
- "we're excited to" (replace with "happy to" or just say what's happening)
- "team" (he is the team — write as himself)
- "robust", "scalable", "next-generation"
- "BrazLatch®" with the ® mark in casual email; just "BrazLatch"

## Structure of a good Alik email

1. **Greeting + thanks** — one line, name them.
2. **One sentence on the product mechanism** that's relevant to *their* channel.
3. **One sentence acknowledging something specific from their message**
   (their company size, market, the product line they mentioned).
4. **One real question** that only a serious distributor could answer.
5. **One concrete next step** — a Zoom slot, a sample shipment, a price list.
6. **Sign-off: "Alik" — first name only.** Below that, in smaller type:
   "Alik Braz · braz innovation · Mikhmoret, Israel · brazlatch.com"

Total: 6–10 sentences. Never more than 12.

## Example of what NOT to do

> Dear Mr. Carter,
>
> Thank you for reaching out to BrazLatch! We're thrilled to hear about
> your interest in our innovative product line. At braz innovation, we
> pride ourselves on delivering best-in-class solutions to the equestrian
> industry, leveraging our patented triple-action mechanism to provide
> unparalleled safety and reliability for your discerning customers.
>
> We'd love to set up a discovery call at your earliest convenience…

That's not Alik. That's marketing.

## Example of what to do

> Hi Ryan,
>
> Thanks for writing in. With 14 stores across NSW you'd see the latch
> sell against the spring-bolts you already carry — same hand operation,
> but it won't open if a horse leans on it.
>
> Quick question that helps me size things up: what's your sell-through
> per store on the spring-loaded latches you stock right now?
>
> If the numbers fit, I'll ship you a sample unit this week and we
> jump on a 20-minute Zoom next week.
>
> Alik
>
> Alik Braz · braz innovation · Mikhmoret, Israel · brazlatch.com

That's Alik.
