## Variable hints

- `{{relevant_feature}}` — pick one and weave it in:
  - "the triple-action — three motions in one pull, impossible to open
    by accident"
  - "the side-changing — same unit reverses left/right, halves your SKU count"
  - "the self-locking spring — closes back on itself with no extra step,
    which kills the most common warranty complaint on this product category"

- `{{qualifying_question_one}}` — pick one:
  - "what's the typical margin you target on hardware closures in this
    price bracket?"
  - "are you running a single brand per category or do you do
    comparison merchandising?"
  - "what's the volume per store / per quarter you'd plan around for
    a first run?"

- `{{qualifying_question_two}}` — pick one:
  - "which existing brands do you stock in this category and where do
    they fall short for your customers?"
  - "are you direct-to-retail or do you wholesale into farm-supply
    chains and rural co-ops?"
  - "do you import direct from manufacturers or work through a UK/EU
    importer-of-record today?"
