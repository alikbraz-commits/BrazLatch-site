## Variable hints

- `{{personal_hook}}` — one short clause referencing their company,
  location, or vertical. Examples:
  - "With your network of feed stores across QLD"
  - "Given Equine NSW's focus on premium hardware"
  - "Working out of Tamworth into the cattle market"

- `{{their_vertical}}` — the channel they wrote in about. Examples:
  - "equestrian retail"
  - "rural hardware co-ops"
  - "agricultural fencing wholesale"
  - "pet enclosure / kennel-run distribution"

- `{{specific_observation_from_their_message}}` — quote or paraphrase one
  thing they actually wrote. *Required.* If they didn't write anything
  specific, ask the user (Alik) for it before drafting.

- `{{qualifying_question}}` — must be answerable only by a real
  distributor. Pick one (don't ask all):
  - "what's your current sell-through per store on the spring-loaded
    latches you already carry?"
  - "which competitor brands have you stocked in this category and
    where do they fall short for your customers?"
  - "how many SKUs of hardware closures do you typically run per store,
    and what's the margin you target on a product like this?"
  - "do you go direct to retail, or do you wholesale into co-ops and
    farm-supply chains?"
