# Qualifying rubric — score new distributor inquiries 0-10

Score each new inquiry on these factors. **Cap at 10.** Anything below 4
gets a polite-but-thin reply or a redirect; anything ≥ 7 deserves
Alik's personal time.

## Scoring

| Factor                                              | Points  |
|-----------------------------------------------------|---------|
| Territory is in our open list (AU/NZ, UK, EU-open, India) | +3 |
| Company name is real (resolves on web search / LinkedIn) | +1 |
| They mention specific channel: retail, wholesale, OEM | +1 |
| They mention store/dealer count or geographic footprint | +1 |
| They mention competing brands or current product line | +1 |
| They mention volume / revenue / forecast              | +2 |
| They write more than 3 sentences in the message       | +1 |
| Email domain matches a business (not gmail/yahoo/hotmail) | +1 |
| Phone number provided                                 | +0.5 |
| Channels listed match our verticals (equine, agri, industrial, DIY, pet, child-safety) | +1 |

**Penalties:**

| Red flag                                                                | Points |
|-------------------------------------------------------------------------|--------|
| Territory is closed (US, CA, established EU)                            | −5     |
| Message reads like generic outreach / "interested in your products"     | −2     |
| Email domain is a free provider AND no company name given               | −2     |
| Asks for distributor pricing in the first message before qualification  | −1     |
| Looks like a vendor pitch, recruiter, or journalist                     | −10    |

## Tiers

- **🟢 Strong (7–10)** — personal reply from Alik this week, sample shipment offered.
- **🟡 Promising (4–6)** — templated reply + one qualifying question.
- **🔴 Weak (0–3)** — polite acknowledgement, no specific offer, add to nurture list.

## Examples (anchors)

> "Hi, I run a 14-store equestrian retail chain across NSW (Equine NSW
> Pty Ltd, equinensw.com.au). We stock spring-bolt and lever-latch
> hardware from Tucker, Hunters and a few Chinese OEMs. Looking for a
> premium product to refresh the category. Volume ~3,000 units/quarter
> across the network. Can we talk?"
> 
> Score: **9** (territory +3, company real +1, channel +1, footprint
> +1, competitors +1, volume +2 → 9; capped at 10)

> "Hi we are distributor in India and interested in your products. Please
> send pricelist."
> 
> Score: **3** (territory +3, distributor-pricing-in-first-message −1,
> generic phrasing −2, free domain unknown, +0 → effectively 0–3)

> "I'm an end consumer in Tennessee and the latch on my barn keeps opening.
> Where can I buy your product?"
> 
> Score: **−5** (territory closed −5). Redirect to National Hardware /
> Lowe's / Amazon.
