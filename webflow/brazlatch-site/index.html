What is BrazLatch

A latch you can trust.
Even when no one is watching.

BrazLatch is a patent-protected mechanism latch with a unique triple-action design. It provides extra safety by preventing any accidental opening, clears the entry, and is easy to operate with one hand. The triple-action mechanism works in a sequence that must be completed consecutively — it's easy to operate with one hand, but cannot be completed by any animal or other force.

01

Self-Locking

The latch returns to its locked state automatically — no extra step, no forgotten gate.

02

Triple-Action

Three sequential motions in one fluid movement. Designed for the human hand, impossible for nudges, gusts, or noses.

03

Universal Fit

One product replaces dozens of single-purpose latches across stalls, gates, fences, trailers and storage.

Seven environments. One latch.

From heavy-duty agricultural gates to a baby-proof cabinet — BrazLatch is engineered for safety, simplicity and strength wherever a reliable closure matters.

  • Farms & Gates

    Heavy-duty steel construction for paddock and pasture gates.

  • Trailers

    Spring-action so the latch self-locks against your livestock — even on rough roads.

  • Stalls & Boxes

    Other latches pop open when a horse leans on the door. This one doesn't — sequence-only release.

  • Fences & Gates

    Triple-action opening — easy from outside, impossible from horse, dog or curious nose.

  • Warehouse & Industrial

    Heavy-duty closure for external storage, equipment cages and security gates.

  • Backyards

    Garden gates, pool fencing, kennel runs — anywhere a reliable, weatherproof latch belongs.

  • Baby & Kids

    Cabinet and play-area gate latches. The release sequence is designed to be hard for small hands and intuitive for adults.

How It Works

One motion. Three actions. Total safety.

1

Installation

Bolts on. Universal mounting holes, swap-side ready out of the box — left or right hinge, no special tooling.

2

Triple Action

Lift · twist · slide. Three sequential motions in one fluid pull, completed only by intent — never by accident.

3

Reversible

The same unit fits left- or right-hand gates. Flip it in the field — no second part, no special tooling.

About the Inventor

Alik Braz — Mikhmoret, Israel.

Since I was two years old I remember myself on our ranch — running around between the horses' legs, climbing from their backs onto the rafters and up to my tree-house, hands always dirty with the smell of fresh feed.

Three things shaped me growing up: horses, building, and mechanics. From a young age I was the one people came to when a tricky problem needed solving in a simple, practical way. Over the years I came to love what I call sophisticated simplicity — the discipline of making a complicated thing easy to use without losing what makes it work.

That's what BrazLatch is. One mechanism, three actions, no extra steps. It came out of my own workshop here in Mikhmoret because the latches we had on the ranch kept failing in ways that mattered. I'd like to share it with people facing the same kind of everyday problems — straightforward solutions, built to last.
