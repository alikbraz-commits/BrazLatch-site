# BrazLatch outreach email template (REVISED — canonical as of 21 May 2026)

This is the user-approved canonical template. Use exactly this structure.

## Subject line

`BrazLatch + {{COMPANY}} — {{TERRITORY_ADJECTIVE}} distribution partnership`

## Body

```
Dear {{COMPANY}} Team,

{{PERSONAL_HOOK}} That made me think there may be a strong fit with BrazLatch™.

BrazLatch™ is a patented self-resetting gate latch with a unique sequential opening mechanism that prevents accidental opening by animals, vibration, or interrupted attempts — while remaining effortless for one-handed human use.

Key reasons it may fit {{COMPANY}}:

✓ {{PATENT_LINE}}
✓ Proven U.S. market presence (National Hardware / Lowe's)
✓ {{FIT_LINE}}

We are currently exploring {{TERRITORY_ADJECTIVE}} commercial partnerships and thought {{COMPANY}} could be a strong fit.

Would you be the right team to speak with regarding new product partnerships, or could you point us to the correct person?

Kind regards,
Alik Alexander Braz
Inventor & Founder — Braz Innovation
Mikhmoret, Israel
```

## Token rules

- **{{COMPANY}}** — exact company name from leads.csv
- **{{PERSONAL_HOOK}}** — one sentence noting something specific about the company (e.g. "I noticed Tigerlink distributes Locinox — one of the most respected patented gate hardware brands in Europe.")
- **{{PATENT_LINE}}** — territory-specific:
  - AU: `Australian patent protection until 2036`
  - UK: `UK patent protection — exclusive position available`
  - EU / Ireland: `European patent protection — exclusive position available`
  - Canada: `Canadian patent protection — exclusive position available`
- **{{FIT_LINE}}** — channel-specific one-liner (e.g. "Strong fit for stable doors, paddock gates, and tack rooms")
- **{{TERRITORY_ADJECTIVE}}** — `Australian` / `UK` / `Irish` / `Italian` / `Spanish` / `Portuguese` / `Polish` / `Czech` / `Hungarian` / `Romanian` / `Canadian`

## Voice notes (revised tone)

- Softer than the first draft — no hard "selecting one exclusive partner" claim.
- "We are currently exploring [Territory] commercial partnerships" — exploratory, not selecting.
- "Could be a strong fit" — not "strongest fit we've identified".
- 3 bullets only.
- "Kind regards" sign-off (not "Happy to schedule").
- Sign as `Alik Alexander Braz / Inventor & Founder — Braz Innovation / Mikhmoret, Israel`.
- "Braz Innovation" is capitalized in this revision (was lowercase in voice-guide.md).

## Provenance

Revised Tigerlink draft provided by Alik mid-session on 21 May 2026 — this version supersedes the earlier longer template.
