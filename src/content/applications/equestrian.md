The original use case. Stable doors, paddock gates, tack rooms — anywhere a curious or determined horse has historically taught its handlers a hard lesson about latch design. BrazLatch's sequence-only release means no nose, no tooth, no leaning shoulder will open it. The rider opens it one-handed with the reins in the other.
