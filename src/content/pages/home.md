## How it works — in one motion.

**Installation.** Bolts on. Universal mounting holes, swap-side ready out of the box — left or right hinge, no special tooling.

**Triple Action.** Push, rotate, release in sequence. Three sequential motions in one fluid pull, completed only by intent — never by accident.

**Side Changing.** The same unit fits left- or right-hand gates. Flip it in the field — no second part, no special tooling.
